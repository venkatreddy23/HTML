Benelli 600i

The Benelli 600i is powered by a 600cc inline-four, liquid-cooled engine, producing roughly 79 bhp and 54.6 Nm of torque. It offers a 6-speed gearbox, dual disc brakes, dual-channel ABS, a 15-liter tank, split seat, digital-analogue display, and weighs approximately 231 kg, with stylish streetfighter design

BMW M3

The BMW M3 is a high-performance sports sedan featuring a 3.0-liter twin-turbo inline-six engine, delivering up to 543 hp (depending on the variant), with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive. It boasts a quick 0-100 km/h time of 3.5 seconds, advanced chassis, adaptive suspension, premium interiors, and impressive handling

Hummer EV

The GMC Hummer EV is a powerful all-electric supertruck offering up to 1,000 horsepower and 15,591 Nm of torque with three electric motors. It features an EPA-estimated range of up to 315 miles, advanced off-road capabilities like CrabWalk and Extract Mode, adaptive air suspension, and high-tech amenities. The SUV version delivers up to 830 hp and 480 km of range, built for extreme off-roading and impressive acceleration (0-60 mph in about 3.3 seconds).
